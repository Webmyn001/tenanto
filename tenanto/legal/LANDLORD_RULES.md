# Tenanto — Landlord Platform Rules

**Version: 2026-04**

These rules are in addition to the User Terms of Service. You must accept these before publishing any listing.

## 1. Verification

- The NIN, utility bill, ownership documents, and selfie you submit must be genuinely yours
- The property you list must be one you legally own or are authorised to lease
- You may be asked for additional verification at any time

## 2. Listings

- All listings must accurately represent the property
- Photos and videos must be of the actual unit being rented (no stock photos, no unit-of-similar-type)
- Minimum: 8 photos, 5 videos
- Address details remain hidden until a tenant pays the inspection fee
- We may auto-flag or reject listings that fail content moderation or appear fraudulent

## 3. Inspections

- You must scan the tenant's QR code at every inspection meeting — this is how we verify the meeting happened
- You must show the actual unit listed (not a different unit)
- If you no-show, the tenant's inspection fee is refunded and your trust score takes a hit

## 4. Payments — the critical clause

**You agree to receive rent payments only through Tenanto.**

This means:
- You will not ask a tenant to pay you directly (bank transfer, cash, mobile money, etc.)
- You will not offer "discounts" for off-platform payment
- You will not share your bank account details with a tenant in chat

If we detect or are credibly told you violated this:
- Your account will be suspended
- Any rent currently held in escrow for your listings may be refunded in full to tenants
- You forfeit pending platform fees and any cashback owed
- We may share evidence with law enforcement if fraud is involved

## 5. Tenancy

- You must allow the tenant to move in on the agreed date
- The unit must be in the condition shown in the listing
- If the tenant raises a dispute within 7 days of move-in, escrow is frozen pending review

## 6. Bank account / payouts

- The bank account you register for payouts must be in your name (matching your verified ID)
- We use Paystack to transfer escrow funds to that account; you'll receive funds usually within 2 business days of escrow release

## 7. Fees

- Tenanto charges 10% of rent as a landlord platform fee, deducted at escrow release
- Optional: ₦5,000 / 30 days for Featured promotion of a single listing
- Optional: subscription tiers (Pro / Enterprise) for unlimited listings + priority placement

## 8. Suspension and removal

- We can suspend you for any of the violations above
- We can remove individual listings without removing your account
- Suspended landlords may not create new accounts; doing so is itself a violation

## 9. Indemnity

- You are responsible for the legal status of your property (title, tax, building approvals)
- You indemnify Tenanto against claims arising from misrepresentation in your listings

By clicking "I agree" you acknowledge that the anti-bypass clause in §4 is the core of this platform's value proposition, and that violating it puts your account, your funds, and your reputation at risk.
